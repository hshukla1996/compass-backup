Do you want us to share your income and household application information with your utility to help enroll your household in a Customer Assistance Program (CAP)?

Many Pennsylvania gas and electric utilities operate CAPs to help low-income customers afford the cost of utilities.

DHS can share your information with your utility to help you enroll. Your information will be kept confidential.

You can get a LIHEAP grant even if you do not want DHS to share your information with your utility for this purpose.

Your utility may need to contact you for more information before enrolling your household in a CAP.

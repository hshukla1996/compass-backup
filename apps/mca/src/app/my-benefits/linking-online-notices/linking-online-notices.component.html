Would you like to receive online notices?(required)
This is required.
  • Get easy access to your notices online
  • Improve your record keeping
  • Help the environment
CHIP notices are not available online at this time.

We will send you a confirmation email if you choose to enroll in online notices. To make sure you receive this email, add donotreply@pa.gov to your safe senders list.

Once you receive it, you must confirm in the email.

If your email is undeliverable, you will automatically be enrolled back into paper notices. Any paper notice we send you through the mail because your email is undeliverable will be a copy of the original notice and will have the same date. You can opt-out of online notices at any time by changing your preference or by contacting your caseworker.

If you choose to go paperless, we will send alerts to your email whenever your notices are available to view online. It is your responsibility to view these notices and share the information with other household members. However, you will need to log into your My COMPASS Account to view the actual notices.

Note: Some notices will not be available online and will be mailed to you.

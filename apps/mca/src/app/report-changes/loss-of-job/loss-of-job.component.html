How to report loss of job.

Follow these instructions to report a job loss.

  1. Download the Employment Termination form.

  2. Add the Name, Case Record Number, and SSN on the form.

  3. Have your previous employer complete and sign the form.

  4. Return the form to your County Assistance Office (CAO) within 10 days using one of these ways:

  • Upload the form to your MyCompass account
  • Email to: {{countyObj[county] ? countyObj[county].email : ''}}
  • Mail to: {{countyObj[county] ? countyObj[county].officeName : ''}}, {{countyObj[county] ? countyObj[county].address : ''}}

If you have questions or need help, you can contact the local county assistance office local county assistance office at: Phone Number: {{countyObj[county] ? countyObj[county].phoneNumber : ''}} Fax Number: {{countyObj[county] ? countyObj[county].fax : ''}}

You can also report this change by having your previous employer write a statement that includes the following information:

  • Employer's Name
  • Employer's address and telephone number
  • Date Employment ended
  • Date of final pay and gross amount of final pay
  • Employer's Signature

Return this statement to your County Assistance Office within 10 days.Make sure you include your name and record number on the statement.

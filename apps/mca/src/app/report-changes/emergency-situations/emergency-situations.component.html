Is your household experiencing one of these emergency situations?

(required)
  • In danger of being without fuel (less than a 15 day supply)
  • In danger of having utility service terminated (you've received a notice that service will be shut off within the next 60 days)
  • Out of fuel
  • The main heating source or secondary heating source (a source that is used to operate the main heating source or used if the main heating source is not working) has been completely shut off
  • Broken heating equipment or leaking lines that must be fixed or replaced

If you're experiencing any of these scenarios and need immediate assistance outside of regular business hours (8AM-5PM Monday through Friday) or if it's a holiday, call the after-hours Crisis hotline at 1-866-452-6152.

If you do not need immediate assistance,please select you can continue your LIHEAP Crisis request. All LIHEAP Crisis requests are addressed within 48 hours of receipt.
